About Walk in the Light
In 1996, a 24-year-old woman began writing to God. She didn't know she was writing a book. She didn't know she was writing for you. Nearly 30 years later, these journals have become Walk in the Light — a testimony that pain has a purpose, perspective brings healing, and God's promises never fail.
